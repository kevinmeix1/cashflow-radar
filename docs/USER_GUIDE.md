# User Guide

CashFlow Radar is designed for small business owners who want a simple answer to: "What should I do today?"

## Dashboard Overview

The top of the dashboard shows six operating signals:

- **Baseline breach**: the first date cash is expected to fall below the safe threshold.
- **After actions**: whether selected actions remove the breach.
- **Revenue upside**: expected revenue from Xero-backed growth opportunities.
- **Protected cash**: projected cash improvement from selected cash actions.
- **Growth actions**: selected revenue actions ready for approval.
- **Data quality**: whether the Xero snapshot is forecast-ready.

## Pending Approval

The approval queue is the main workflow.

Each selected action shows:

- **Xero evidence**: the invoices, contacts, service categories, or amounts that triggered the recommendation.
- **On approval**: what the system will queue next.
- **Human control**: what the owner reviews before anything external happens.

Use the checkboxes to include or exclude actions. The forecast and approval totals update from the selected actions.

## Revenue Opportunities

The revenue opportunity agent looks for practical growth moves:

- reactivating high-value dormant customers
- converting repeated ad-hoc work into retainers
- recommending adjacent services to existing customers
- recovering cash from likely late payments
- repackaging weak services into stronger offers

These are intentionally grounded in Xero activity rather than generic sales advice.

## Cash-Flow Actions

Cash-flow actions are ranked by expected impact before the risk window:

- invoice chase
- early-payment incentive
- supplier payment delay

Each action includes confidence, relationship risk, rationale, and a message draft.

## Human Approval Queue

The communications panel contains agent-drafted messages. They are drafts only. A business owner should review tone, relationship context, and commercial terms before sending.

## Xero Connection

If Xero credentials are not configured, the app uses seeded demo data. To connect live data, follow [Developer and Xero Setup](DEVELOPER_GUIDE.md).
